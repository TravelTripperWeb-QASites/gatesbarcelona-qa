Key West Cuisine

best Restaurants

Key West offers an incredible range of culinary styles, incorporating incomparably fresh seafood and a mix of culinary practices traditional and progressive. Local favorites include Hogfish — a Key West-specific catch — and Key Lime Pie, an island classic. Across the board, Key West restaurants serve a balance of standard seafood dishes and more progressive creations.
Guests at The Gates have the luxury of exploring this culinary landscape at the Blind Pig — our backyard tapas food truck — and at an array of other great Key West dining destinations.

Key West golfing

Key West Golf Club

South Florida is synonymous with great golf. With that in mind, The Gates has partnered with the Key West Golf Club, the only Caribbean golf course in the United States. Players of all abilities will be challenged on the 18-hole, 6,500 yard tropical course masterfully designed by golf legend Rees Jones. The course is home to the famous “Mangrove Hole,” a 143 yard par 3 that is played over a field of thickly intertwined tropical mangroves.
Guests of The Gates are sure to enjoy an exhilarating round as well as special rates.

Key West beaches

Beach paradise

Key West isn’t your classic Caribbean beach destination, where thousands upon thousands of visitors crowd cabanas and jockey for space on the sand. Instead, Key West beaches offer a certain aura of cozy exclusivity. Spending a day on a Key West beach with the sand, water, and sun as company is a relaxing and restorative experience: Soak it up.
